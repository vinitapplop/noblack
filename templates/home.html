Dear Citizen of India, First time I see any leader steps ahead for change in true spirit. The true strength of a country is best demonstrated by the willingness of its own citizens to accept the change. It is high time to support the movement of Honorable PM Modiji for demonetization of 500/1000 notes. To keep moral high of the general public, is the most critical thing as of now. Please volunteer for above contribution at www.noblack.in. You can search the other people working in the same area and can jointly do better.

We can contribute in the following ways:-
  • To help people who are in need of money for health issues.
  • To help people who are in need of money for marriage or any other function.
  • To provide water to people in queue.
  • Assisting them in filling up forms and guiding them how to deposit/withdraw/exchange.
  • To guide provisions of income tax and impact on deposit.
  • Free health checkup by doctors few days till situation gets normal.
  • To boost the moral of the people who are queue though slogans or picture or national Flag.

Please remember your one step can change the entire world. "ASHA SE AAKASH THAMA HAI" Lets blow the wind of hope! Jai Hind!
